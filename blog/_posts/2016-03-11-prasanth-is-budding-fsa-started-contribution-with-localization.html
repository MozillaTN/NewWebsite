6) How did you discover the Web?

During my childhood,it was my elder sister, who introduced me to web, by calling me to accompany her, whenever she went to internet cafes.

7) How did you hear about Mozilla and Mozilla Tamilnadu Community?

I heard about Mozilla during a tech meetup in Madurai. I was introduced to Mozilla TamilNadu Community by Gautham Venkat, a fellow FSA from Amritha University.

8) How and why did you start contributing to Mozilla?

It all started in localisation event which took place during inauguration of MozMadurai.

I started contributing to Mozilla because I felt that such contribution would have a great positive impact me. As I felt , I have grown my skills over the past months.

10) What’s the contribution you’re the most proud of?

The best contribution that I made is “The privacy campaign” which I conducted in my college.

11) What advice would you give to someone who is new and interested in contributing to Mozilla?

The common problem faced by the begineers is hesitation to mingle. Never hesitate to ask doubts. Because with each doubts , we get each new ideas.

12) If you had one word or sentence to describe Mozilla, what would it be?

I will describe Mozilla as “the best community for learning and teaching as well!”

13) What exciting things do you envision for you and Mozilla in the future?

I could see Mozilla community growing in a rapid pace. With more events coming up, more contributions generated, there comes more innovation. Mozilla will strive hard to keep the Web open and I could see myself helping for it’s great cause.

14) Is there anything else you'd like to say or add to the above questions?

Nothing more.Start working today! and make a better tomorrow.
